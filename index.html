About

The gaming industry has gained a serious momentum in recent years. Because of these reasons, We want to create a game. Because, We are having fun while We are playing video games. So, what is the OPERATION- V Game? OPERATION-V is a free-to-play 2D BASED tactical video game developed by TAB GAMES. TAB's meaning is (Taylan-Alperen-Burak) who are the founders of the company.

In the game, You have a Spacecraft and you can control it! On the other hand, There is a ball and It always moves along the screen such as from left to right as continuously. The target which players want to shoot it is the ball in the game. There is a counter and it counts the time spent in the game and besides, when you shoot the fire, Also the counter increases one.
